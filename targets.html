GOAL NO 04
QUALITY EDUCATION

Education liberates the intellect, unlocks the imagination and is fundamental for self-respect.
It is the key to prosperity and opens a world of opportunities, making it possible for each of us to contribute to a progressive, healthy society.
Learning benefits every human being and should be available to all.

Targets

  • Free primary and secondary Education
  • Equal access to quality pre primary education
  • Equal access to affrodable technical,vocational and higher education
  • Increase the number of people with relevent skills for financial success
  • Eliminate all dscrimination in education
  • Universal literacy & numeracy
  • Education for sustainble development & global citizenship
  • Build and upgrade inclusive & safe schools
  • EXpand higher education scholorship for Developing contries
  • Increase the supply of qialified teachers in developing countries
